Core Features

Built for Clinical Impact

Every feature is purpose-built to support — never replace — the clinical judgment of emergency professionals.

01

Structured Intake

Enter vitals (HR, BP, SpO2, temp, pain) alongside free-text chief complaints for comprehensive patient assessment.

02

AI Triage Engine

LLM-powered ESI prediction (1–5) using chain-of-thought reasoning grounded in official ESI clinical guidelines.

03

Factor Analysis

Interactive bar chart showing exactly how each vital and complaint keyword pushed the acuity score up or down.

04

Live Patient Queue

Kanban-style dashboard with all patients color-coded by ESI level. At-a-glance department situational awareness.

05

Simulation Mode

Auto-generates realistic patient scenarios for training, demos, and evaluation — no real data needed.

06

Reasoning Trace

Watch the AI think step-by-step through each clinical case in real-time. Fully transparent, fully auditable.

→ Step 1: Assessing immediate life threats — Patient presents with chest pain, diaphoresis. HR 112 bpm (tachycardic). SpO2 91% (hypoxic). This combination suggests possible acute coronary syndrome.
→ Step 2: Evaluating high-risk indicators — Age 72 with acute onset, radiating pain to left arm. BP 158/94 (hypertensive). These are classic ACS red flags requiring immediate evaluation.
→ Step 3: Resource estimation — Will require: ECG, troponin labs, chest X-ray, IV access, continuous monitoring, likely cardiology consult. Resource count: 6+ → exceeds ESI-3 threshold.
→ Step 4: Final determination — High-risk presentation with vital sign abnormalities and multiple resource needs. Patient meets criteria for ESI Level 2.

How It Works

From intake to insight in under 5 seconds.

1

Patient Data Entry

Nurse enters vitals and chief complaint via structured intake form.

2

Clinical Prompt Construction

App builds a structured prompt embedding ESI guidelines and patient data.

3

AI Analysis

Claude processes the case through 4-step chain-of-thought clinical reasoning.

4

Results Rendered

ESI level, confidence score, reasoning trace, and factor analysis displayed.

5

Queue Updated

Patient card added to the Kanban board, sorted by acuity for department-wide visibility.
